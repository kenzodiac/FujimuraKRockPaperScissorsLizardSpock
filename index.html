Rules: How to Play

"Rock Paper Scissors Lizard Spock" is an extremely stupid game designed to "improve" upon the classic "Rock Paper Scissors". The same general rules/logic applies, only there are more play-options.

Each option can defeat two other options, and be defeated by two other options. The heirarchy is as follows:

  • [🪨 Rock]: crushes [✂ Scissors]; crushes [🦎 Lizard]
  • [📄 Paper]: covers [🪨 Rock]; disproves [🖖 Spock]
  • [✂ Scissors]: cuts [📄 Paper]; decapitates [🦎 Lizard]
  • [🦎 Lizard]: eats [📄 Paper]; poisons [🖖 Spock]
  • [🖖 Spock]: smashes [✂ Scissors]; vaporizes [🪨 Rock]
In this game, you can choose between "2-Player" and "Vs Computer" modes. In each, you can play a "Best of 5", "Best of 7", and a Sudden Death mode. Each requiring 4, 3, and 1 round-wins in order to claim victory.

Enjoy the game, and "Live Long and Prosper"!
